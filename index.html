How it works

Enter your Employee ID and press Generate (or hit Enter).
A valid UPC-A is created by padding the ID to 11 digits and calculating the check digit (the last digit) using the GS1 standard.

Download

The badge is downloaded as a JPG image (3000 × 3000).
The file is saved to your device’s Downloads folder.

Privacy

No data is stored, uploaded, or shared.
Everything runs locally in your browser.
